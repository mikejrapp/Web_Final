The Ranger

The ranger is probably the most mobile unit at your disposle. He is fast and has a good range for attack. Unlike the warrior or the rogue, the ranger cannot take damage, but is capable of dealing it.

Stats

  • Health: 250
  • Attack: 150
  • Action Points: 4
  • Move Speed: 3
  • Attack Range: Ranged (no less than 2 tiles no more than 3)

Special Abilities

  • Overdraw

    • The ranger can hold their turn to overdraw his bow. He cannot move or attack the same turn but will deal his damage * 1.5 on succesful hit next turn. This ability costs 4 action points.

  • Exert

    • The ranger may move an additional 2 tiles this turn at the cost of his attack. This ability costs 1 action points.
