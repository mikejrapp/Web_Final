The Warrior

The warrior class is the tank. He doesn't do a lot of damage, but he can take it. When you need someone to get in the enemies face or draw their attention, he's the man for the job.

Stats

  • Health: 1000
  • Attack: 50
  • Action Points: 3
  • Move Speed: 2
  • Attack Range: Melee (one tile)

Special Abilities

  • Taunt

    • This ability forces enemy characters within range to attack this character if able. This ability is used in place of an attack and costs 2 action points.

  • Block

    • The warrior has a chance to negate up to 100% of incoming damage from one enemy character. This ability is used in place of an attack and costs 2 action points.
