The Mage

The mage is physically weak but more than makes up for that with her magical ability. Useful as a healer or for controlling the flow of combat, the mage will prove valuable in all scenarios. Just keep her out of combat or you'll lose her.

Stats

  • Health: 200
  • Attack: 50
  • Action Points: 3
  • Move Speed: 2
  • Attack Range: Ranged (no less than 2 no more than 3)

Special Abilities

  • Heal

    • The mage can heal her companions from a distance up to 25% of their current health. This ability is used in place of an attack so the mage can move the same turn. Heal costs 2 action points.

  • Paralysis

    • The mage is able to paralyze her opponents for up to 2 rounds. This ability is used in place of an attack so the mage can move the same turn. Paralysis costs 2 action points.
